1. Server Rules

  • Do not abuse commands e.g. !unstuck or find/use exploits to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Do not advertise other servers.
  • Do not ask for a rank up; be it respected, mod, admin or VIP.
  • Do not block spawn.
  • Do not harass or bully other players.
    • Doxing (revealing a players real-life identity) a player will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • Do not intentionally annoy people for your own amusement.
  • Do not metagame. This means giving away other playersâ€™ positions through chat, Steam, third-party programs etc.
  • Do not prop spam.
  • Do not spam your microphone (this includes continuous noises, talking in a loud manner, playing music through your microphone).
  • Do not use cheats, hax, scripts or any other type of game modification. Doing so will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • Players must not attempt to impersonate Prop Hunt staff and/or players in anyway (unless you have permission).
  • Prolonged periods of inactivity will result in being kicked.
  • Threatening (or undertaking) to DDOS the server or any of our players will result in a permanent ban from the Monumental Community.
  • When casting a vote kick, discuss why in chat first and give time for the person being kicked to respond e.g. Votekick on Ph33x for prop spam, any objections?
    • Do not start a vote if reasonable objections are raised.
    • Do not start a vote if staff are online.
  • When using any donatorr commands (!gag, !mute) you must inform staff via admin chat (@) before doing so.

2. Useful Information

  • If no staff are online and an individual is breaking the rules, record or screenshot their behaviour and post a ban request on our forums.
  • Players can activate god mode to avoid being killed by typing !god in chat. If you have god mode, you are not to attack anyone.
